Overview

World War Two is a world conflict that lasted from September 3rd, 1939 to September 2nd, 1939. It was continued from disputes from World War I. It is considered the deadliest war in the history of the war, resulting in 50M live taken

Aftermath

The war ended in 1945 with the unconditional surrender of the Axis powers. The world witnessed the devastation of the Holocaust and the first use of nuclear weapons. The conflict reshaped borders, accelerated decolonization, and led to the founding of the United Nations.
